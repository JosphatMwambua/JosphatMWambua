I am a mathematician and incoming Ph.D. student in Mathematical Sciences at Clemson University. My interests lie at the intersection of computational mathematics, numerical optimization, statistics, and data science.

## Current focus

My recent research examines Newton–Raphson, modified Newton-type, Gauss–Newton, and Levenberg–Marquardt methods for logistic regression models, with particular attention to numerical stability and ill-conditioned problems.
